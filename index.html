About

The Game was built by Lana AboRaya and Daniel Aspir.
The goal of the Game is that the spaceship should eliminate all the bad chickens.
Every five seconds the "bad" chickens will accelerate their speed.
The "bad" spaceships can "shoot" bullets at the player's spaceship.
> If a ball fired from a bad chicken manages to hit the player's spaceship,
it is disqualified and returns to the starting point at the bottom end.
The player will be allowed 3 disqualifications until the end of the game.
We encountered some difficulties when building the chicken invaders game,
such as, resolution, results from a spaceship impact with a chicken and
building the leaderboard.
